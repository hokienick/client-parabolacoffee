# Parabola Coffee Roasting Co.

## Business
- **Type:** Neighborhood specialty coffee shop + roaster
- **Location:** 3504 Adams Ave, San Diego, CA 92116 (Normal Heights / North Park)
- **Hours:** Mon–Sun, 7:30 am – 3:00 pm
- **Primary goal:** Drive foot traffic + local brand love; secondary: ecommerce (roasts, merch, gifts)
- **Mission:** Specialty Latin American gourmet coffee; supports minority-owned farms; community-first

## Register
brand

## Audience
San Diego locals, late 20s to early 40s, value craft + authenticity + community. Know their coffee. Respect local businesses. Not corporate-coffee people.

## Aesthetic Direction
Dark-ground warm modernism. Near-black body with amber accent. Editorial display type (Cabinet Grotesk for display, Geist Sans for body). Confident asymmetry. Feels like a vinyl record shop that also roasts great coffee — deliberate, local, a little loud.

## Design Dials
- DESIGN_VARIANCE: 7
- MOTION_INTENSITY: 6
- VISUAL_DENSITY: 5

## Color Strategy
Committed — dark ground carries the brand. Amber accent (not beige/espresso/oxblood) brings warmth without cliche.

- Body: Geist Sans (regular/medium) — next/font/google

## Anti-Patterns (do not use)
- No cream/beige/sand background
- No gradient text
- No identical card grids
- No numbered section markers
- No em-dashes anywhere
- No AI purple glow
